My Dream House

My dream house would have a small garden whereI will grow my own vegetables and fruits. The house that I fancy would be considerably big with four rooms and a spacious common area. My dream house should be comfortable for my parents, grandparents and siblings. It has lots of rooms and windows and big red front door. There is a huge living room with comfortable sofas and enormous TV screen on the wall for my family and I to spend time together. In the basement, there is a giant game room with a ping-pong table and drum set for me and my friends.
